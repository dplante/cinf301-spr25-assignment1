Fun Facts

  • Pride and Prejudice: The book Elizabeth is reading in the opening scene is calles First Impressions, which is the original title Jane Austen gave Pride and Prejudice
  • Interstellar: To make the location feel authentic, director Christopher Nolan planted 500 aces of corn to shoot the farm scenes, and then sold the corn after filming for a profit of $162,000
  • La La Land: Ryan Gosling practiced learning piano 6 days a week for 2 hours a day to avoid using a double in the piano scenes
