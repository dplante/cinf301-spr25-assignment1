Fun Facts

  • One of the first ever successful video games was Pong, released in 1972 by Atari.
  • The highest-selling video game of all time is Minecraft, with over 300 million copies sold.
  • Mario was originally called Jumpman in Donkey Kong and was originally a carpenter in that game before being the iconic plumber known today.
  • The PlayStation 2 is the best-selling console of all time, with over 155 million units sold.
  • Studies suggest that playing video games can improve hand-eye coordination and problem-solving skills.
