ABOUT

Formula One (F1) is the world's premier motorsport competition, featuring high-speed, open-wheel race cars designed for extreme performance and precision. Governed by the FIA, F1 consists of a global series of Grand Prix races, where top teams like Ferrari, Mercedes, and Red Bull compete for the Drivers' and Constructors' Championships. With cutting-edge aerodynamics, hybrid power units, and advanced race strategies, F1 pushes the limits of engineering and driver skill. The sport is known for its iconic circuits, intense rivalries, and thrilling overtakes, making it the pinnacle of motorsport.
